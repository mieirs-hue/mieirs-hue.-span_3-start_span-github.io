Deployment Sequence

Engineering process from arrival to verified operation.

Measure physical constraints, identify routing conflict points, and capture baseline camera and sensor data.

2. Deploy and Calibrate

Install grid markers, map ORB/ORD features, and tune alignment until localization remains stable under motion.

3. Verify and Hand Off

Run validation routes, document calibration values, and transfer an operational field map to your team.
